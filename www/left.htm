Welcome to the data.table project!

Fast subset, fast grouping and fast ordered joins in a short and flexible syntax, for faster development.

Example: data[a>3,sum(b*c),by=d] where data has 4 columns (a,b,c,d).

  • 10+ times faster than tapply()
  • 100+ times faster than ==
